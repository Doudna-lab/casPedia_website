Contact


Have questions or want to contribute to casPEDIA?

Please follow the directions below to ensure your request is processed as efficiently as possible. Additional information is available in the FAQ section.


BECOME A CUTATOR

We are grateful for your interest--casPEDIA is a community resource and your participation is welcome! Anyone can contribute to the database. If you would like to become a curator (i.e. create and edit wiki content), send a message to with the subject header "Request to Become General Curator" and a small summary of your research and/or CRISPR experience. Our team will followup with next steps on how to formalize the process and guage your interest on specific wiki features that need your attention.

REQUEST A NEW ENTRY

If you would like to suggest a new casPEDIA entry, contact caspediaadmins[at]google.com with the subject line "Request for new casPEDIA Entry: [New Entry Name]." Novel cas nucleases will be considered for the wiki, if the enzyme's function is described in 1 or more peer-reviewed publications and there is sufficient information to complete the webpage. We are also happy to work with CRISPR pioneers with papers under submission/revision and are willing to contribute to the curation process themselves. If you are unable to curate content for the entry you suggest, please identify a minimum of 5 scientist who'd be able to assist with the entry. The casPEDIA Consortium will review all potential wiki content to maintain the database's curation standards.

REQUEST NEW FEATURE

If you have an idea for a new feature to support our user base, please let us know. Contact caspediaadmins[at]google.com with the subject line "Request for new feature," and include a brief description.

REQUEST EDITS

If you are interested in updating a preexisting entry, contact caspediaadmins[at]google.com with the subject line "Request for to update Wiki." Please describe the reason for the update and any citations from literature that are relevant. Our team will review your message and reach out with details regarding next steps or the results of the update. If you find an error in an entry, please include a description, citation supporting your requested amendment and relevant screenshots of the issue.

EXTENDED DATA REQUESTS

If are are interested in acquiring any data that isn't available on the Downloads Page [[Add link]], please contact caspediaadmins[at]google.com.

BUG REPORTING

casPEDIA is still under construction and we appreciate bug reports! Bugs can be reported by contacting the team with the subject header "Bug Report." A screenshot of any errors is strongly encouraged, along with a description on any user-activity that elicited the error.
